Adversity reveals my true nature

Welcome back to Journal Category.

Today is Monday – the first day of a week, I – Leona – hope you have a great deal of energy that is going to lead you to the best productivity.

Before jumping to the main topic; I want to let you know that I have been practicing contemplation for a week.

I think I have paid more attention to myself thanks to practicing this.

I am close to getting knowledge of what I like and do not like as well.

This morning; while reading Daily Stoic, which is separated into 336 parts, corresponding to 366 days in a year; the title is like a reminder of what I have experienced when I was in an unpredictable situation.

The title is “Adversity reveals”.

Now we move to all my reactions while reading today’s contemplation.



What did the book say to its readers?

In a short term, this article said that the way you handle unpredictable situations reveals who you are, what your true nature is, and your natural reactions.

For example; you are in a date with your new girl in a restaurant, you are together romantically.

Looking at the menu then you choose orange juice for her and coffee for you.

The waiter, however, instead brings orange juice and coffee, he brings tea and coffee.

Then you let your anger out and rude the waiter.

The girl coming with you watches all of that, then she decides to break up a day later.

That will be the trouble that adversity causes to us if we are without practicing to control our reactions in unpredictable circumstances.

So, these are stuff that Daily Stoic desires to tell you today.

Now we are going to delve deeper into this by coming to my own experiences.



My real example of how terrible those experiences was?



In case you do not know me (of course not because I am no one); I was an overconfident and stubborn person.

Unfortunately, no one knew who I was, but my parents.

Listening to everybody is not an option to me, I thought I was better than them, whenever I was wrong, that was not my fault (that was my thought about 10 years ago).

I would have got a better spirit of learning if I had not been overconfident.

In choosing high school time; my parents and teachers gave me so much advice that I should choose a medium school instead of a high level school.

Finally, I chose the one that had been in one of the ten best schools in my city.

Yep! you may know the outcome: I entirely failed.

The failure of not having a chance to learn in one of the best schools was the first huge adversity in my life…because of me.

Now; take a look at the title and guess what the next one is.

You are right, the emergence of my true nature rose.

I let my anger free, spreading widely to my loved people.

Besides, there was the most important mistake that has been a game changer of my life until now.

I lost the motivation of attempting to reach the peak that I had wanted to.



How negative have I become?

Following the story I mentioned above, you can see that I created a negative me.

I gave up on everything that I had had faith in, including my path of education.

I almost lost my chance to attend the national high school exam with a grade 2.9 in history.

My teacher didn’t let that happen, she saved my ass.

In the period, I missed all the opportunity to make a better life such as: attending to English club (English is my best and I love English too), joining to football team of my school (one of my biggest dreams – become the best goalkeeper in the world, or at least become a No.1 GK og my national football team), etc.

It was not until my wife and I had a daughter that I realized that I need to let all of my failures go, which have pulled me down in all those years.

After nights of contemplation, I have become enlightened that having control in unpredictable circumstances plays a key role in bringing happiness to you and your loved one as well.

Let’s think about your hard day at work, when you come home you are upset about your bad day.

Then you realize that that dinner still has not been prepared.

Again, you let the anger out, pour all of this on your small family who are the only one by your side.
